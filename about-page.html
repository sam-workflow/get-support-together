WHAT IS GET SUPPORT TOGETHER?

Introducing a New Path to Mental Health Access

Get Support Together is a comprehensive directory connecting you with everything—from youth empowerment programs to crisis psychiatric care. We host therapists, psychiatrists, support groups, resource centers, and more—right where you live, and now virtually too.

We believe you deserve to see all your options, not bits and pieces. So we’re building a platform that lays them out clearly—type, cost, intensity, credentials—so you can choose what feels right for you.

Why it Matters

After decades of watching loved ones struggle, facing “Why is getting well so hard?” ourselves, we realized many people are left with only painful choices:

Taking a chance on a provider who may not be a good fit

Getting a referral based on someone else’s experience—not yours.

Going to emergency care simply because there was no earlier support

Wandering through websites, diagnosing from Dr. Google, overwhelmed by choices

Why Get Support Together, now?

Because getting well shouldn’t be so hard.

This project comes from lived experience: decades of watching loved ones struggle to find help and facing the same barriers myself. Too often, the only options were chance referrals, emergency rooms, or endless self-diagnosis. We’re here to change that by making care options clearer, more accessible, and easier to trust.

We built Get Support Together to offer a different path. One where you can compare your options, see what aligns with your needs, and make the next right step with confidence.
